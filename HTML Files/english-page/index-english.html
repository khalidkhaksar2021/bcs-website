Computer Science Department which came into existence in 2013 at paktia university, education faculty. that currently has 5 Master teachers and 159 male and female students

Computer Science Deparment Vision:

Computer science department has a resolution to increase their advancement to Faculty in near future in order to train professional students. and to be changed to a strong center in training and research field, until the graduates of this department would be eligible to be effective in information technology field, social problem solving, and also economical grow.

The computer science department has to be a high quality department for afghanistan new technology development, expansion and be responsive to the needs of the society. this department should perform its rule in community economics, in country restfulness, and also in respect to the holy directions of islam. Finally, spread modern knowledge and get international recognition.

Computer Science Department Mission

For all the citizens of the country that fulfill their requirements will be provided equally access to the higher education, preparing educational and modern programs that assures high quality elaboration and research. Provide better services, and train such kinds of specialists that they can participate as an active part in national economy, be effective in social advancement and nation arrangement, and become an enhancement for our country.
